🔒 Privacy Policy

Last Updated May 27, 2026

Welcome to DocProTools (https://docprotools.space). Your privacy is not just important to us — it's built into our technology. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your data when you use our document editing tools.

📌 The Most Important Thing You Need to Know:
We do NOT upload, store, or have access to any of your files. Every document, image, and PDF you process stays 100% on your own device. Zero server uploads. Zero data collection.

1. Data Collection & Processing

We collect NO personal data or file content. Here's what that means in practice:

  • No file uploads: All tools (PDF merge, split, compress, rotate, PNG to JPG, image conversion, etc.) run locally in your browser using WebAssembly technology.
  • No account required: We don't ask for email, name, or any registration.
  • No tracking cookies: We don't use Google Analytics, Facebook Pixel, or any third-party trackers.
  • No server logs: We don't store IP addresses or access logs on our servers.

2. How Local Processing Works

All our tools use modern web technologies (WebAssembly, JavaScript) to process your files directly inside your browser. Your files never:

  • Leave your computer/device
  • Get uploaded to any server
  • Get stored in any cloud
  • Get seen by anyone (including us)

This means even we, the website owners, cannot access your documents or images. What happens on your device stays on your device.

3. What We Do NOT Collect

  • ❌ File names or content
  • ❌ IP addresses
  • ❌ Browser fingerprints
  • ❌ Location data
  • ❌ Email addresses
  • ❌ Usage analytics
  • ❌ Cookies of any kind

4. Essential Technical Information

While we don't collect data, your browser may temporarily store files in memory (RAM) during processing. This is required for the tools to work. Once you close the page or navigate away, everything is permanently erased from your device's memory.

💡 Technical note: Some tools may use browser cache to load assets faster. This is standard web behavior and does not contain any of your personal files or document content.

5. Third-Party Services

We do NOT integrate with any third-party analytics, advertising networks, or external APIs that could track you. Our website runs completely independently with no external JavaScript that collects data.

However, if you click external links (like our GitHub repository or social media profiles), those sites have their own privacy policies. We recommend reviewing their policies separately.

6. Children's Privacy

Our tools are safe for users of all ages because we collect no data. We do not knowingly or unknowingly collect any information from children under 13. Since no data collection occurs, there is nothing to delete or manage.

7. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or our privacy practices, you can reach us through:

  • Contact form: Available on our homepage at https://docprotools.space (scroll to "Get In Touch" section)
  • GitHub: Issues or discussions on our repository

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy occasionally to reflect improvements or legal requirements. The "Last Updated" date at the top of this page will always show when changes were made. We encourage you to review this page periodically, but since we collect no data, significant changes that affect you are highly unlikely.

9. Your Rights Under GDPR / CCPA

Because we collect absolutely no personal data, there is no data to access, correct, delete, or export. Your rights are fully protected by design — no action needed from you or us.

🛡️ Summary: We built DocProTools with a "privacy by design" philosophy. Every tool is engineered to work completely offline after the initial page load. Your files never leave your browser. You don't need to trust us — the technology proves it. Open your browser's Developer Tools (Network tab) while using any tool — you'll see zero file uploads to any server.
